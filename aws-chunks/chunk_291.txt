. IAM Access Analyzer AWS Identity and Access Management Access Analyzer (IAM Access Analyzer) can help you identify resources that are shared with an external entity. You can also use IAM Access Analyzer to validate IAM policies against policy grammar and best practices, and generate IAM policies based on access activity in your AWS CloudTrail logs. IAM Access Analyzer uses logic-based reasoning to analyze resource policies in your AWS environment, such as bucket policies.
