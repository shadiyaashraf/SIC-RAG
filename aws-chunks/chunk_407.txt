Authorization requests can be made by principals within your AWS account or from another AWS account that you trust. Once authorized, the principal can take action or perform operations on resources in your AWS account. For example, the principal could launch a new Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud instance, modify IAM group membership, or delete Amazon Simple Storage Service buckets. Tip
