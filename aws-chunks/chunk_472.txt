. Grant least privilege When you create IAM policies, follow the standard security advice of granting least privilege
, or granting only the permissions required to perform a task. Determine what users and roles need to do and then craft policies that allow them to perform only those tasks. Start with a minimum set of permissions and grant additional permissions as necessary. Doing so is more secure than starting with permissions that are too lenient and then trying to tighten them later.
